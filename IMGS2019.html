IMGS 2019

The International Medical Geogrpahy Symposium in Queenstown, NZ.

It was great privilege to both attend and present at IMGS 2019. Not only was the conference in a location that will be hard to ever rival, it was a hugely informative. The conference allowed me to make great connections in the UK and further abroad, with potential collaborations on the horizon.
I enjoyed having the opportunity to present my research to such a specialist and knowledgeable audience. The ensuing conversation around using commercial smartphone data to monitor activity highlights the potential of my data and analysis in extending the current sphere of knowledge.
