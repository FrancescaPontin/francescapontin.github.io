Fran Pontin

ESRC funded PhD student at the Unviersity of Leeds.
Based in Leeds Institute for Data Analytics and the School of Geography and part of the Data Analytics and Society Centre for Doctoral training. My reserach utilises data from smartphone apps to investigate the demographic and geographic determinant of physical activty levels in the UK.
