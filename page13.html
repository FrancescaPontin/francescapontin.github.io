Fuell App Launch: Encouraging LIDA to be more active

July 2018

Having won a previous Fuell challenge, whilst beta testing the app for my data partner, my prize of 10 Garmins and a 3-month Bounts subscription seemed like the ideal opportunity to get the office more active. Hence in early July 12 weeks of activity challenges were made available to LIDA office members and associates. The Garmins were lent to office members who did not already own a tracker, with the option to also use the accelerometer and GPS built into smart phones. Alongside the big 12-week challenge, where the prize is a high-end Garmin watch the participants are also competing in fortnightly challenges designed to gently increase the number of steps taken or 'active minutes' completed each day,
