ISPAH Early Career Network Event

October 2018

The Sunday before the start of the full Internaitonal Society for Physical Activity & Health (ISPAH) international conference the Early Career Network met for a career and networking event. In a jam packed day we were lucky enough to get advice from over 20 speakers, covering career pathways and next career steps, to tips for getting published, advice onsecuring funding. As well as a brillaint activity lead by Fiona Bull, programme manager in the department of Prevention of noncommunicable Disease at the WHO, on how to best advocate for physical activity.

Loving the additions to the new version of Mobirise web dev app. It's a great, cleanly designed, user-friendly, not-bloated design program. So easy and a pleasure to use
